Summary Findings: Latitudes vs Maximum Temperatures


As we might expect, this plot seems to be showing us that as we move further north in latitudes, our maximum temperatures drop drastically; nearly a 100 degree drop from 20 degrees latitude to 70 degrees latitude. There is also a lovely slight curve showing us the beginnings of the same pattern as we fall into southern latitudes.
