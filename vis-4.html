Summary Findings: Latitudes vs Wind Speeds


We can see basic patterns in wind speed from this graph, for example that wind speeds topping 20mph seem to be outliers in that they do not occur as often. There does seem to be a higher likelihood that higher wind speeds would occur in latitudes north of the equator, however there is not enough of a correlation in this dataset to say that definitively.
